# Couch Clicker Atomic

<img src="images/couch-clicker-atomic.png" alt="Couch Clicker Atomic" style="float: right; width: 150px">

Build your own mechanical key remote control!

Details on how to build one are at [Couch Clicker Atomic](https://github.com/retsyx/CouchClickerAtomic).

The four activity buttons correspond to the first four activities, long pressing the buttons correspond to the next four activities.

## Pairing

1. In the Amity `Remotes` management tab, press the `Pair` button.
2. Place the remote close to Amity, and press and hold the `Power` and `Volume Up` buttons for 6 seconds.

## Unpairing

Press and hold the `Power` and `Volume Up` buttons for 6 seconds.
